IT and Network Readiness

The next few questions are about your internet setup. Before you start this section, ask someone at your store who handles the internet or tech devices to help you answer the questions.

Note: This person/company would NOT be Comcast, AT&T, or whoever you pay for your internet.

This would be an IT vendor, someone related to you, or yourself if you're in charge of IT/Support - whoever you call when your internet or computers stop working.

If there are no available cable holes, you will need to gather the WiFi information.
